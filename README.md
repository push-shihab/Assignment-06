DigiTools, A React-based digital products web app where users can browse and load digital tools, add them to a cart, and proceed to purchase, all in a clean and interactive experience.

Technology Used: React, JavaScript, Vite, CSS, HTML, DaisyUI

Features

1. Add to Cart — Users can browse digital products and add their preferred items to the cart with a single click.
2. Remove from Cart — Items can be easily removed from the cart, giving users full control over their selection.
3. Proceed to Purchase — Users can finalize their selection and proceed to buy the digital products seamlessly.
